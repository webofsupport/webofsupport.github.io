The Initiative

The Kitchener Waterloo Youth Orchestra is going silent. We're here to stop that.

The orchestra is facing a $10k funding gap. Without it, 150 students lose their music education. Web of Support was built to solve this.


We build professional websites for businesses like yours or individuals like yourself. Instead of paying a traditional agency, you pay the orchestra, so 100% of the profit is donated to help the orchestra.
