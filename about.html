Who We Are

We are a collective of developers saving the music, one website at a time.

Web of Support is a non-profit fundraising initiative. The Kitchener Waterloo Youth Orchestra has provided music education for over 25 years, but budget cuts threaten to close its doors forever.

Our solution is simple: We use our professional web development skills to generate revenue. Instead of profit sharing, 100% of our net proceeds go directly to the orchestra's operating fund.

When you work with us, you aren't just getting an agency-quality website. You are buying violin strings, renting rehearsal halls, and keeping the music playing for 150+ students.
